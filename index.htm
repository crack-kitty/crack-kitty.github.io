Obituary for Meaghan J. Fickett (1984 - 2024):

Passed Away: November 1, 2024 (Age 39)

Biography:

Some knew her as “Meaghan”, but to those who truly loved her, she was an unforgettable force of nature. She lived life to its fullest and always sought adventure and excitement in everything she did.

Born on January 14th, 1985, Meaghan was the youngest daughter of Dennis and Jean Halsdorff, both of whom have predeceased her. She was also preceded in death by her sister, Erin Halsdorff; close cousin, James Gibbons; grandparents John and Helen (O’Malley) Gibbons, and Benjamin and Gloria (Menard) Halsdorff; and many aunts, uncles, and cousins.

Meaghan was a loving mother to her son, Hunter Oldenburg of Oxford, MA. She is also survived by her brother, Dennis Halsdorff II of West Brookfield, MA; sister, Colleen Palacios, her husband Anthony, and their daughters Clover and Winter, all of Brookfield, MA; close cousin, John Gibbons and his family, of Spencer, MA, and parents-in-law, Mark and Teresa Grubbs of Brookfield, MA.

Survived by:

Predeceased by:
